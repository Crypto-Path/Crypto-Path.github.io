4-Letter Words

4-Letter Words is a word game developed by Cyphe Mercury.

Originally a joke that so many bad words are 4 letter words, 4-Letter words strives to help peaople discover new four letter words. These four letter words make you looks smarter now that you don't have to use the bad words to get your point across.

4-Letter Words features a local leaderboard, and fun gameplay. Inspiration was take from the Popcap game, "Book Worm"

Prosody

Prosody is a 4k VSRG developed by Cyphe Mercury. But what is a 4k VSRG?

A 4k VSRG (Vertical Scrolling Rhythm Game) is a type of rhythm game that is played on a keyboard with four arrow keys, arranged in a vertical line. The game consists of notes scrolling down the screen and the player must hit the corresponding arrow key when the note reaches the bottom of the screen in time with the music.

Prosody will feature a diverse library of music, ranging from popular songs to original compositions, created by and for the comunity. The game will include multiple difficulty levels for each song, allowing players of all skill levels to enjoy the game.
